No one reads terms of service agreements, yet we agree to them every day. We routinely trade our rights for the convenience of new features and a rich user experience without a second thought. What happens when we are asked to surrender our rights to use public spaces rather than apps and websites? This is the question that Agree & Continue asks by presenting users of public spaces with terms and conditions agreements that push the boundaries of acceptability.

Agree & Continue is a project by Kris Blackmore and Simon Boas.
